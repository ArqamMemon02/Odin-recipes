Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of flat, wide pasta sheets alternated with rich fillings. Typically, the fillings include a meat or vegetable ragù (a type of sauce), a béchamel or ricotta cheese mixture, and various cheeses like mozzarella and Parmesan. The dish is baked in the oven until the top layer of cheese is melted and bubbly, creating a deliciously hearty and comforting meal. Lasagna is known for its rich flavors and textures, making it a popular choice for family gatherings and special occasions. Variations of lasagna can include different types of meats, vegetables, and sauces, allowing for a wide range of creative interpretations of this beloved dish.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or Italian sausage: 1-1.5 pounds
  • Onion: 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Garlic: 2-3 cloves, minced
  • Tomato sauce: 2 cans
  • Tomato paste: 1 can
  • Crushed tomatoes: 1 can
  • Water: 1/2 cup
  • Sugar: 1-2 teaspoons
  • Dried basil: 1-2 teaspoons
  • Dried oregano: 1-2 teaspoons
  • Salt: to taste
  • Black pepper: to taste
  • Red pepper flakes: to taste
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

    Prepare the Meat Sauce:

  1. In a large pot, cook 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage with 1 chopped onion until the meat is browned and the onion is tender.
  2. Add 2-3 minced garlic cloves and cook for another minute.
  3. Stir in 2 cans tomato sauce, 1 can tomato paste, 1 can crushed tomatoes, 1/2 cup water, 1-2 teaspoons dried basil, 1-2 teaspoons dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer on low heat for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

    5. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:

  5. In a bowl, mix 16 ounces ricotta cheese, 1 egg, 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt until well combined.

    6. Preheat the Oven:

  6. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  8. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  10. Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese over the ricotta.
  11. Repeat the layers (meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella) until you run out of ingredients, ending with a layer of meat sauce and a final sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.

    12. Bake:

  12. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (spray with cooking spray to prevent sticking).
  13. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  14. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  15. Let the lasagna sit for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
