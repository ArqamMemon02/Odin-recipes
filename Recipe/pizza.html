Pizza

Description

Pepperoni pizza is a popular and classic type of pizza known for its bold flavors and crispy texture. It features a traditional pizza base made from a yeast-leavened dough, which is rolled out and topped with a rich tomato sauce. Over the sauce, a generous layer of mozzarella cheese is added, creating a gooey and melty foundation for the main topping: pepperoni.


The combination of the tangy tomato sauce, creamy melted cheese, and the spicy, savory pepperoni makes pepperoni pizza a favorite among many pizza lovers. It's often enjoyed with a sprinkle of crushed red pepper flakes or a drizzle of olive oil, and pairs well with a variety of beverages

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110째F or 45째C)
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 2-3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni (about 30-40 slices)
  • Optional: crushed red pepper flakes, dried oregano, or fresh basil for garnish

Steps

    Prepare the Dough:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, dissolve 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast and 2 teaspoons sugar in 1 1/2 cups warm water. Let it sit for about 5 minutes, or until it becomes frothy.
  2. Add 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons olive oil, and 2 teaspoons salt to the yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5-7 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic. Add more flour if needed.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.

    5. Prepare the Tomato Sauce:

  5. In a bowl, mix 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce with 2 teaspoons dried oregano, 1 teaspoon dried basil, 1 teaspoon garlic powder, 1/2 teaspoon onion powder, 1/2 teaspoon sugar, and salt and pepper to taste. Set aside.

    6. Preheat the Oven:

  6. Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up as well.

    7. Assemble the Pizza:

  7. Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two equal portions if you are making two pizzas.
  8. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough into a circle or rectangle, about 1/4-inch thick.
  9. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a parchment paper-lined baking sheet or a pizza peel if using a pizza stone.
  10. Spread a thin layer of the prepared tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  11. Sprinkle a generous layer of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  12. Arrange the sliced pepperoni evenly over the cheese.

    13. Bake

  13. If using a pizza stone, carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated stone in the oven. If using a baking sheet, place it directly in the oven.
  14. Bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

    15. Serve

  15. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  16. Slice and serve hot, garnished with optional crushed red pepper flakes, dried oregano, or fresh basil if desired.
